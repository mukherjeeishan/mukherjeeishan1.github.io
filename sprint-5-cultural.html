Sprint 5 Cultural

Throughout my foundations progress I have continued to learn more and more about myself and my study habits through the core learning sections. These learnings I have made have had a significant impact on the way I approach my learning. I feel that the biggest change that has occurred for me is that I tend to plan out my learning much more effectively and manage my time better. Instead of jumping into something new by simply starting an exercise I know take the timer to look at the learning objective as a whole and plan out how much time I would like to spend on different sections.
I have learnt that I work well in short bursts and that it is important for me to take regular breaks in order to be most efficient. I have also learned the importance of having a stable and healthy body and mind before you sit down to work. This is the key to getting effective use of your time.


If anything surprised me about the core learning it was probably the overall effect, it has had on my work and study habits. Initially when approaching the core, I found that I was doubting the content and had the opinion that it was not going to do much for me and that it was just an admin part of the course for me to complete however after completing foundations I can say that this is not true at all. The most challenging aspect was the parts of the core that got you to complete self-reflection. I found this hard because it requires you to know you well (especially for strengths and weaknesses).
